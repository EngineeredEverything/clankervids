Where can I watch BattleBots?
BattleBots airs on Discovery Channel in the US. Previous seasons are available on streaming platforms. ClankerVids curates the best highlights, compilation videos, and community content from Reddit's r/Battlebots and YouTube channels.
What weapons do battle bots use?
Common battle bot weapon types include: Spinners (horizontal/vertical/full-body), Flippers (pneumatic/hydraulic), Crushers/Clamps, Hammers/Axe bots, Saws, and Wedges. Spinner robots are currently dominant in the competitive meta due to their destructive potential.
How heavy are BattleBots robots?
The main BattleBots competition uses the 250-pound (heavyweight) class. There are also lighter weight classes in the amateur community: antweights (1 lb), beetleweights (3 lb), hobbyweights (12 lb), and featherweights (30 lb).
